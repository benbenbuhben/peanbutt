Cost = depreciation + fuel/electricity + insurance + WA registration + maintenance. Depreciation is modeled as compounding (10-yr value retained ≈ the 5-yr retention squared) and maintenance as ~2.4× the 5-year figure to reflect rising out-of-warranty upkeep, both are rough, so treat the total as a ballpark, not a quote.
